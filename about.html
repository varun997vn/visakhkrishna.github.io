About

Dec 6th 2021 4 Min read

Welcome to my website. I envision this space for long-form writing based on the subjects I work with and the stories I find fascinating. I hope you find them interesting.

I am a consultant working with problems in planning, operation and maintenance of railway and infrastructure assets. I am based in Stockholm with roots in India, having earned my PhD in Engineering Mechanics in Sweden. I decided to create an online place bringing all my research and blog-posts in one place. You can read them here.

When I am not doing the above, I am dabbling with travel, reading books, history, and a lot more. You can read my stories and travelogues here.
